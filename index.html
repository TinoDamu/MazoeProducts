About

Mazoe Syrups are a rainbow range of flavoured cordials available in 1 and 2 litre packs for tasty refreshment. They are available in Blackberry, Mango , Lime , Cream Soda, Raspberry and Peach flavours. They come either in 1 litre or 2 litre containers. They are great for serving with or without meals, at home and during social gatherings. With Mazoe Syrups, a little goes a long way so you get more refreshment at an affordable price which is good value for money.
